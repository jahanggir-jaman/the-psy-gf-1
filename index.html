Learn More About Jason's Abilities

Jason has an extraordinary ability to connect for readings without ever meeting, seeing, or knowing a person. He excels in phone readings anywhere in the world using his multiple psychic tools to connect to past, present, future or with a spirit who has passed.
Channeling

Channeling means opening yourself up to universal energies and letting them flow through you. It is a way of translating universal knowledge for guidance.

Clairaudience

Clairaudience is a psychic ability whereby you can receive intuitive information from your Higher Self, heaven, and members of your Spirit Guides, angels, via hearing.

Clairvoyance

Clairvoyance is the ability to gain information about an object, person, location, or physical event through extrasensory perception. Any person who has such ability is a clairvoyant.

Palm Readings

Palmistry, or chiromancy, is the of characterization and foretelling the future through the study of the palm, also known as chirology, or in popular culture as palm reading.

Psychometry

Psychometry (from Greek: ψυχή, psukhē, “spirit, soul” and μέτρον, metron, “measure”), also known as token-object reading, or psychoscopy, is a form of extrasensory perception. The ability to hold an object and receive information.

Tea Reading

Also known as tasseomancy or tasseography. This involves reading and decoding information from leftover tea leaves, coffee grounds or wine sediments for symbols, pictures, omens or unusual patterns. Tea leaf reading can be traced back to Asia, Ancient Greece, and the Middle East.

Tarot

The tarot (/ˈtæroʊ/; first known as trionfi and later as tarocchi, tarock) is a pack of playing cards, used from the mid-15th century in various parts of Europe. Tarot card readings help you to find answers about love, relationships, your career, finances and more.

About Jason: The Psychic Godfather

Jason was the psychic for the Mob and now he's here for YOU!
The Psychic Godfather™ has been reading since he was 12 years old

He started working in his family’s Tea Room in 1977, when he was 20.

The first time he shared his gift with his parents, he was still young enough to play with toys like Elsie the Cow and got a vision of a family friend in the neighborhood. "My mother had a friend Elsie,” Jason remembered. "I loved Elsie the Cow. One day, I told my mother Elsie was here and she died."

There was just enough time for a look of bewilderment to come over my mother, Claire, before the phone rang. It was Elsie’s husband. She had hanged herself in the basement of her home. She suffered from depression and could not deal with life after shock treatment at Parkside Nursing Home, in the Bronx.

Jason's mother reacted calmly saying "You are unique, Jason but let’s not mention this to your father." He went on to play with his slinky.

Jason has been gifted with clairvoyance and clairaudience. He is well versed in psychometry, channeling, palm and tea leaf reading. While he can easily use tools like cards, pendulums and crystal balls, he does not need them.
